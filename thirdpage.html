Chocolate Cake

Chocolate Cake Details

Dough:

The dough for the chocolate cake includes a mix of all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, unsweetened cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, salt, eggs, whole milk, vegetable oil, pure vanilla extract, and boiling water. Optional: chocolate chips for added richness.

Filling:

To prepare the cake, preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans. Sift together the dry ingredients, add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the mixture. Gradually add boiling water, mix well, and fold in chocolate chips if desired. Bake for 30-35 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean. Allow the cakes to cool before removing from pans.

Topping:

For the topping, use your favorite chocolate frosting, such as chocolate buttercream or ganache. Decorate the cake with additional chocolate shavings, sprinkles, or fresh berries for a delightful finishing touch.
