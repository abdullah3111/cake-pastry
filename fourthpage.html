Macaroon Details

Dough:

Macarons feature a delicate and smooth exterior made from a mixture of almond flour, powdered sugar, and whipped egg whites. The batter is piped into small circles, creating the iconic cookie shells.

Filling:

The filling between the macaron shells adds a burst of flavor and texture. Popular filling options include ganache, buttercream, jam, or flavored creams. Creatively pair different flavors for a unique and delightful experience.

Topping:

While macarons typically do not have a traditional "topping," the decorative and flavorful aspect often comes from the colorful and creative shells. Experiment with food coloring to achieve vibrant hues and present the macarons on a decorative plate or in a box for an elegant touch.
